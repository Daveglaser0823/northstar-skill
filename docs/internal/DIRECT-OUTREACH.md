## Context

If the LinkedIn post gets < 1,000 impressions by Day 7, the problem is discovery, not product.
The fix is direct, targeted outreach to founders Dave already knows.

**Who to message:** Founders in Dave's network who:
- Run a SaaS or subscription business (Stripe is almost certain)
- Use Shopify (ecommerce founders)
- Have mentioned OpenClaw, AI agents, or automation
- Are in the indie hacker / founder community on LinkedIn

**Message volume goal:** 10-15 personalized DMs. Not a blast. Not a template spam.
5-10 of these converting to installs would be meaningful early signal.
